Arthur Hague is the founder and designated broker of Hague Creek Land Brokerage. For over two decades, Arthur has specialized in navigating the specific nuances of raw acreage and rural property transactions across Arizona. His deep practical knowledge of groundwater regulations within Active Management Areas (AMAs), water rights transfers, grazing leases, and topographic boundary surveys sets his advice apart from residential real estate agents.

Under his guidance, Hague Creek has evolved into a premier land marketing agency, leveraging cutting-edge drone video systems, coordinate-free topographic map integrations, and high-impact printable property books to present Arizona land listings to qualified buyers nationwide.

Our Philosophy

Why We focus On Land

Land is not just an asset; it is a permanent foundation. Standard real estate services often try to fit land listings into residential templates, completely missing essential property data like soils, access easements, mineral rights, and zoning restrictions. We built Hague Creek to represent land as a distinct, specialized asset class, requiring detailed analysis and comprehensive presentation.

Our marketing platform operates with complete transparency and due diligence, providing buyers with downloadable property sheets containing accurate parcel reports, maps, and zoning details.
